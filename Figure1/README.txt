**Fig1_compare_TADs.R** 

This script computes the Measure of Concordance between TAD partitions.
See methods section in Donaldson, Sungalee, Zufferey, Tavernari et al. for details.

**Fig1_compare_compartments.R** 

This script computes the fraction of bins that are assigned to the same compartment for pairs of Hi-C matrices.
See methods section in Donaldson, Sungalee, Zufferey, Tavernari et al. for details.
